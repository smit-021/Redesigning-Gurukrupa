About Gurukrupa

21 years ago, when Gurukrupa was founded, we had just one goal in our minds.

Customer-first isn’t just something we say lightly, it’s our culture, the way we work. Or as we like to say, we’re people in the people-first business. Understanding their needs and developing solutions that are tailor-made for those unique experiences, proactively anticipating future needs and poising ourselves to best deliver and always rating ourselves as per how happy our consumers are.
